VISION

Our vision stretches beyond conventional automation. We're building towards a future where 70% of warehouse and factory automation in South Asia will run on RigBetel-derived systems, making India a global hub for ROS-compatible robotics hardware.

Born in July 2020 at MIT Pune, our journey began when three engineers identified a critical gap: India's heavy reliance on imported automation solutions was holding back innovation and accessibility. What started with ₹18 lakh in competition winnings has evolved into a pioneering force in robotics, serving 25 countries across ASEAN and MENA regions.

Our early challenges shaped our innovative approach. When conventional LIDAR sensors failed in dusty Indian factory environments during our first field tests, we developed a proprietary multi-sensor fusion algorithm combining mmWave radar and stereo vision. This practical problem-solving mindset continues to drive our development of robust, real-world solutions.

Today, we're not just building robots; we're nurturing an ecosystem. Through our open-source contributions, educational initiatives, and modular platforms, we're making advanced automation accessible to enterprises of all sizes while cultivating the next generation of robotics talent.
