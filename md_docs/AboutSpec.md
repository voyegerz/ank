# About Us Page Specification - ANK Corporate Website

## Overview
The About Us page provides an in-depth look at ANK's mission, team, and engineering capabilities. It follows the "Amwerk" style with large typography, background image sections, and a clean professional layout.

## Page Structure

### 1. Title Hero Section
- **Headline:** "About ANK" (Superheadline) / "Who we are?" (Main Headline) / "About us" (Outline/Background text)
- **Subheadline:** "We help our clients to seamlessly move programs through design and production."
- **Background:** Large industrial/office background image with a dark overlay (`post_18.jpg` equivalent).
- **Styling:** Left-aligned text, large font size, outline text effect.

### 2. Stats/Counters Section
- **Grid:** 4-column layout.
- **Counters:**
    - World awards (30+)
    - Project success (99%)
    - Expertise (60+)
    - Years of Excellence (15+)
- **Styling:** 
    - Animated counting effect.
    - Large font size in accent color (Sky-500).
    - Headline and subheadline for each stat.
    - Negative top margin to overlap with the hero section.

### 3. "Who is ANK?" Centered Section
- **Layout:** Centered content with background image overlay.
- **Superheadline:** "Who is ANK?"
- **Main Headline:** "A multidisciplinary team providing cutting edge solution, design, development & manufacturing services."
- **CTA:** "Join us on a project" button.
- **Styling:** High contrast (White text on dark/industrial background).

### 4. Detailed Services List
- **Layout:** 2-column grid of detailed service blocks.
- **Content:** 
    - 01 Parts production: Quick change, optimal testing procedure.
    - 02 Precision & quality: Dramatically underwhelm meta-services.
    - 03 Auto & aero manufacture: Infomediaries for high-end production.
    - 04 Quality replacement parts: Phosfluorescently provide access.
- **Styling:** Numbered list with dash separators and "Read more" icons.

### 5. Our Team Section
- **Grid:** 4-column profile grid.
- **Profiles:**
    - Profile Photo.
    - Role (Superheadline).
    - Name (Headline).
    - Social Media Icons (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn).
- **Styling:** Hover effect on photos (Zoom-in).

### 6. Careers Block
- **Layout:** integrated into the Team section as the 4th box.
- **Content:** "Interested in joining ANK?" / "Simply fill out the Careers form..."
- **CTA:** "Apply now" button.
- **Styling:** Blue overlay background with white text.

## Technical Requirements
- Same as Home page (Framer Motion, Lenis, Responsive).
- Animated counters component.

## Assets Needed
- About hero image.
- Team photos (Sawyer, Sue, Matthew placeholders).
- Middle section background image.
- Careers background image.
