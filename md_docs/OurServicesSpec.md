# Our Services Page Specification - ANK Corporate Website

## Overview
The Our Services page (and sub-pages) provides a comprehensive list of ANK's engineering and automation solutions. It follows the "Amwerk" style with checklist benefits, pricing plans (if applicable), and detailed service grids.

## Page Structure

### 1. Title Hero Section
- **Headline:** "Understanding your needs" (Superheadline) / "Our services" (Main Headline) / "Amwerk" (Outline/Background text)
- **Subheadline:** "Continually market-driven best practices for your engineering requirements."
- **Background:** High-quality industrial image (`post_16.jpg` equivalent).
- **Styling:** Left-aligned text, large font size, outline text effect.

### 2. Values / Checklist Section
- **Layout:** Two columns.
- **Left Column:**
    - Small Label: "ANK VALUES" (Uppercase, tracked).
    - Headline: "Engineering the products."
    - Checklist (Lucide CheckCircle2 icons):
        - Solutions for your manufacturing business.
        - Production of bespoke tools and machines.
        - Designing, building and testing with highest quality.
        - Delivering the best results for reasonable cost.
    - CTA: "Find out more" button.
- **Right Column:**
    - Large image (`post_17.jpg` equivalent) with a negative margin for a layered effect.

### 3. Service Categories Grid
- **Grid:** 4-column layout with hover effects.
- **Items:**
    - Metal forming and processing.
    - CNC machinery made products.
    - (Include current project services here: CAD Design, Software Development, etc.)
- **Styling:** Image-based cards with title and description appearing on hover or in a clean layout.

### 4. Job Done Section
- **Layout:** Split section (Image left, Content right).
- **Content:**
    - Small Label: "WHO WE ARE"
    - Headline: "ANK always gets the job done"
    - Subheadline: "Distinctive potentialities in engineering and automation."
    - CTA: "Find out more" button.
- **Styling:** Gray background for content column.

### 5. Payment Plans / Solutions Section (Optional/Context Dependent)
- **Grid:** 3 columns.
- **Cards:**
    - Plan Name (e.g., "Start with ANK", "Full Service", "Advanced").
    - Price (Amount).
    - Features List.
    - CTA: "Get it now" button.
- **Styling:** Distinctive cards, one highlighted as the "Premium/Popular" choice.

## Technical Requirements
- Reusable `ServiceCard` and `ChecklistItem` components.
- Responsive grid layouts.

## Assets Needed
- Hero background image.
- Side image for values section.
- Split section image.
- Pricing card background images (if using price lists).
